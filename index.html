What you will learn

Groundwater level mapping

Produce piezometric surface maps and interpret spatial gradients for flow direction analysis.

Fluctuation mapping & prioritization

Analyse seasonal and long-term fluctuations, generate pre- and post-monsoon difference maps, and prioritize critical zones for management.

Recharge zone identification

Integrate geology, slope, LULC, drainage density, and soil data through multi-criteria evaluation and remote sensing to delineate recharge zones.
